There are 2 types of instructions accepted
  • Category --> represented in a single line.
  • Item details --> represented in format -- item,price, description

For Example
Soup
Chicken Corn Soup,$5.99,Soup made of chicken in chicken stock
Vegetable Soup,$4.00,
Hot and Sour Chicken Soup,small = $5.99;large = $10.99,Soup made of chicken in soy sauce

Noodles
Penang Asam Laksa,$8.95,"hot & sour lai-fun noodles in lemon grass fish broth with fish, onion & pineapple"

"price","item","description" --> on line 1 can change the order in which items are set
